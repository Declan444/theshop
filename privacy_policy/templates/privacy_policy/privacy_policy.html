Privacy Policy Equestrian Online

Effective Date: 03/01/25

Welcome to Equestrian Online! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, address, email, phone number, and other details provided during checkout or account creation.
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed through Stripe, a secure third-party payment processor. We do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Order History: Details of the products you purchase.
  • Newsletter Sign-Up: Your email address if you opt to receive marketing communications.
  • Cookies and Usage Data: Information about how you use our site, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including delivery.
  • Communicate with you about your orders and account.
  • Send marketing emails (if you have subscribed to our newsletter).
  • Improve our website and services by analyzing user behavior through cookies.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Newsletter Sign-Up

By signing up for our newsletter, you agree to receive updates about new products, promotions, and events. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us at info@equestrianonline.com

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us:

  • Recognize returning visitors.
  • Personalize content and offers.
  • Track website performance.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party providers to offer secure services, including:

  • Stripe: To process your payments. You can read Stripe's Privacy Policy here: https://stripe.com/privacy.

We ensure that any third parties handling your data comply with GDPR requirements.

6. GDPR Compliance

As a business based in the EU, we adhere to the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal obligations (e.g., tax record-keeping).
  • Right to Data Portability: You can ask us to provide your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of receiving marketing emails or object to certain types of data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@equestrianonline.com

7. Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously and use industry-standard security measures to safeguard it. While no system is completely secure, we strive to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law (e.g., tax records).
